FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Support
Do I need experience to start? Beginner friendly

Not at all. We’ll assess your current level and guide you through technique and progression from day one.

Can I try a session before joining? Yes

Absolutely. Send us a message and we’ll schedule a visit or a trial session based on availability.

What should I bring? Essentials

Training shoes, a water bottle, and comfortable clothing. Towels are recommended.

Are memberships cancelable? Flexible

Yes. Plans can be changed or canceled depending on your billing period. Contact us and we’ll help you out.

Do you offer personal training? 1:1 coaching

Yes. Personal training is available with tailored programming and regular progress check-ins.

How busy is the gym? Peak hours

Evenings are typically busier. If you prefer a quieter session, late mornings and early afternoons are best.
